OUCH

Open UC Hardware Advice

Sharing UC expertise on Open Source Hardware.

This website is a collection of resources and advice on Open Source Hardware in research, teaching, and practice, from the perspective of UC professors and graduate students. Here we compile materials on crafting tutorials for open source hardware projects, post recorded talks from conferences and workshops related to open source hardware, and present interviews with UC experts on open source hardware and its impact.
